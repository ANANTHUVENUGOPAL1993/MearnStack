Design of Machine Elements | 4th Edition

Author: V B Bhandari

Pages-944

This book provides a systematic exposition of the basic concepts and techniques involved in machine design. Throughout the book SI units have been followed and the BIS designation of materials and components used. Design principles have been explained in detail and exhaustively illustrated through solved examples.

Strength of Materials

Author:Debabrata Nag

Pages-600

The subject lays the ground for various Engineering subjects, ranging from Machine Design, Finite-Element Analysis, Theory of Structures, Bio-Mechanics, and Fracture Mechanics. In this book, the topics are broadly divided into two parts: Elementary Strength of Materials and Advanced Strength of Materials, thereby progressing from basic fundamentals to detailed analysis.
